            Decision Factors
            Why Notion over alternatives
                  Considered Obsidian but found it too complex and difficult to set up ‣
                  Notion offered seamless Google Calendar integration without requiring migration of existing
                  calendar data ‣
                  Familiar interface similar to Google Docs and Sheets made learning curve minimal ‣
                  Visual appeal and aesthetic design compared to Google's "boring" interface ‣
                  Influenced by YouTube content creators using Notion ‣
                  Needed something quick to implement during second semester of high school without time
                  to "restart whole planning design" ‣
            Trigger for paid purchase
                  Hit file upload limitations on free plan when trying to upload images for film project ‣
                  Had already invested time learning the platform and felt comfortable with it ‣
                  Didn't want to switch software after becoming familiar with Notion ‣

            Product Limitations & Challenges
                  Interface can become cluttered and difficult to navigate with extensive use
                  Requires being "very organized and on top of things" to use effectively
                  No built-in templates - users must create custom systems (e.g., spreadsheets with custom
                  code for to-do lists)
                  Adding entries to custom spreadsheets is cumbersome
                  Sometimes created multiple spreadsheets tracking same information, leading to inefficiency
                  Switched back to Google Sheets for budget tracking due to complexity

            Feature Discovery
                  AI features were discovered after purchase, not part of initial decision ‣
                  AI capabilities proved valuable for generating spreadsheet structures and planning
                  frameworks
                  Travis noted they "wouldn't have set up the planning for language learning or travel in the
                  same way" without AI assistance to define parameters ‣
                  AI doesn't auto-fill content but helps create structure - users still need to input their own data
